Starting Your Research:

  • Gather information about your family
    • -What do you already know about your family history?
  • Talk to your relatives
    • -Conduct oral interviews
  • Write everything down
    • -Start with downloading family charts and pedigrees
  • Go Online
    • -More information on websites on the tips page!
  • Find specific websites
    • -Based on your heritage you can find specific sites


  • Find a family history center near you
    • -If you are in the Kansas City area-check out Midwest Genealogy Center
  • Organize your information
    • -Find more iformation on the Documenting page
  • Plan your next steps
    • -Start planning a trip to see where your ancestors came from
  • Share your research
    • -There are many platforms to share your information
